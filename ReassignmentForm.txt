<tr><th bgcolor="white"><font color="black" size="2"><center><p>
The following conditions apply to this reassignment and must be acknowledged by all parties:<br><br>
1. This reassignment is <b>effective immediately</b> upon receipt of this document unless otherwise stated by the issuing Command officer.<br><br>
2. If the <b>relevant Head of Department is unavailable</b> at the time of reassignment, the assignee will be held in a provisional capacity and formally reviewed upon the Head of Department's return to duty.<br><br>
3. The assignee is expected to <b>report to their new department promptly</b> and make themselves known to their supervising Head of Department at the earliest opportunity.<br><br>
4. Any <b>equipment, access, or materials</b> associated with the previous role must be returned to the issuing department before assuming new duties.<br><br>
5. This reassignment is subject to a <b>provisional review period</b> not exceeding one standard shift, after which it may be confirmed, amended, or revoked at Command's discretion.<br><br>
6. <b>Refusal to comply</b> with this reassignment order without written grounds submitted to Command constitutes insubordination and will be handled accordingly.
